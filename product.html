LINK & ECOG

ECOG - Electrocorticography uses surface electrodes that are surgically placed on the surface of the brain to allow the machine to read and interpret brain signals. Since these electrodes are placed inside of the skull, they can transmit and receive better signals than if they were on the outside of the head.

1. BCI scale

BCI can analyze Neurons, at one time. Obviously, the more neurons that the BCI can analyze the faster and more accurately the machine will respond.

2. BCI Resolution

BCI devices analyze signals with details. Essentially, the spatial resolution is a measure of closely the machine can tell you about the activities of each individual neuron.

3. Surgery & Equipment

Some BCIs have very low invasiveness and require almost no physical equipment to be attached to the body but other BCIs actually require chips to be placed on the brain so they can read the brain signals.
